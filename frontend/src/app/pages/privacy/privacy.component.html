Privacy

Privacy Notice

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide, such as your name, email, phone number, property address, and preferences. We also collect basic usage data to help improve the platform.

How We Use Information

  • Match sellers with relevant agencies and agents.
  • Provide access to dashboards and lead updates.
  • Improve platform performance and security.

Sharing Information

Seller lead details are shared with the assigned agency and agents only. We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with service providers who help us operate the platform.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely and access is restricted by agency. We apply technical and administrative measures to protect information from unauthorized access.

Access and Correction

You can request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us. We respond within a reasonable time as required by Australian privacy law.

Complaints

If you believe your privacy has been breached, contact us first. If unresolved, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

Contact

If you have questions, email support@leadexchange.au.
